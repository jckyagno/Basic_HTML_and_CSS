HERBS

Green Herb

One of three different herbs found growing in Pots in and around the Village, Castle and Island areas. The most useful of the three herbs you can find. The Green Herb by itself will restore a small amount of health, but this effect can be enhanced by combining it with the Red Herb to create the Mixed Herbs (G+R).

Red Herb

One of three different herbs found growing in Pots in and around the Village, Castle and Island areas. A herb that has no use on its own, but enhances the effect of the Green Herb when combined.

Yellow Herb

One of three different herbs found growing in Pots in and around the Village, Castle and Island areas. This herb can be used to increase your maximum health, allowing you to take more damage without being killed. If you combine this with the Red Herb and Green Herb, it will increase your maximum health and restore your health completely.
